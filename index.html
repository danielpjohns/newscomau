Celebrity Life

Newcastle neighbours claim rock star Daniel Johns and pop princess Jade Thirlwall are expecting — and the renovations tell the whole story

Sources allege the couple are five to six months along and expecting a girl, as locals say the seaside city is quietly overjoyed

Staff Writers • news.com.au • 15 Mar 2026, 9:42am
f 𝕏 ✉

Daniel Johns (centre) pictured with girlfriend Jade Thirlwall (left) and what appears to be one of the tradies working on the couple's Newcastle property (right). Picture: Supplied

It started as renovation gossip. Now it's the rumour Newcastle can't stop talking about.

Neighbours living near the property being extensively rebuilt for former Silverchair frontman Daniel Johns are claiming the rock recluse and his pop star girlfriend Jade Thirlwall are expecting their first child together.

And according to multiple sources, they're around five to six months along.

The alleged pregnancy comes as locals say the scale of the rebuild — described by one neighbour as "basically a full teardown" — suddenly makes a lot more sense.

"New rooms, completely new layout. But now that we're hearing about the baby, it all adds up."

The tradie who said too much

The rumours gained significant traction last week when a tradesman working on the site allegedly let something slip at a nearby café.

According to a witness at the scene, the builder was overheard saying the nursery was "the trickiest bit" of the job before apparently realising what he'd revealed.

"He went quiet straight away, like he knew he'd said too much. But everyone at the table had already heard it."

"They're building a forever home, they're having a baby — Daniel isn't the kind of man to do things by halves."

— Source close to the couple

A girl, sources say

Adding fuel to the fire, an insider claiming to be close to the couple alleges the pair are expecting a daughter.

"They've known for a while and they're absolutely over the moon."

Neither party has confirmed or denied the reports.

The sighting that started it all

The speculation intensified after the couple were spotted together inspecting the property alongside a builder, with witnesses describing the pair as openly affectionate throughout.

"Daniel had his arm around her the whole time. They were holding hands walking through the rooms."

It was during that visit that witnesses say Jade's pregnancy became difficult to ignore.

"There was no hiding it, especially side-on. A proper bump. She looked absolutely amazing — genuinely glowing."

The ring question

With a baby allegedly on the way and a forever home taking shape, speculation has now turned to whether wedding bells could also be on the horizon.

No ring has been publicly sighted. But sources are hinting there could be more news to come.

Jade has been conspicuously absent from social media in recent weeks, with no close-up photographs posted since the renovation rumours began circulating — a notable shift for someone who has historically been active online.

Newcastle reacts

Perhaps the most telling detail in all of this is the reaction from the city itself.

Newcastle has watched Daniel Johns since his teenage years — through the extraordinary highs of his Silverchair fame and the long, difficult years that followed. The news that he may be entering the happiest chapter of his life has reportedly moved many locals deeply.

"He went through so much. To see him happy — really happy — with someone who clearly loves him and a baby on the way? It's just lovely. Newcastle looks after its own."

Another simply said: "He deserves this."

News.com.au has contacted representatives for both Jade Thirlwall and Daniel Johns for comment.
